Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address
  • Course of Interest
  • Any additional message you include

This information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries. We do not share this information with third parties or use it for any form of push marketing.

Website Analytics

We use a privacy-focused, self-hosted analytics tool. Here's how it protects your privacy:

  • No personal data is collected: It does not collect or store any personal information about visitors.
  • Anonymized data: All visitor data (i.e. pages visited, time spent on the website, browser and device type etc.) is anonymized to ensure your privacy.
  • No cookies: It does not use any type of cookies. (Cookies are small files stored on your device that websites use to remember information about your preferences or activity. Since our analytics do not use cookies, you can browse our site without worrying about these tracking mechanisms.)

This data helps us understand how our website is used and improve its content and performance, all while ensuring your anonymity.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide through the contact form is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Providing you with information about our courses and services

We do not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes unless explicitly requested

Your Data Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@vvidhya.com.

Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to other websites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Please note that we do not operate these external websites and are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

Third-Party Services

Our website utilizes third-party services for hosting, analytics and performance optimization. All these services follow industry-standard security protocols to protect your data. While these services may process technical data (e.g., IP addresses, browser types, device types etc. ) to enable the functionality of the website, no personal information is stored or shared.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This privacy policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website regarding the information they share and/or we collect on this website. It does not apply to any information collected offline or through channels other than this website.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. Contact form data is stored securely and deleted once it is no longer required.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with the date of the latest revision.

Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
